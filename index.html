Campus Life

Library

was established in 1930

he Great Falls Library is a warm and welcoming place for students, teachers, and parents. It is the goal of the library program to develop and encourage a love of reading for all students and assist them in becoming critical thinkers who are effective and responsible users of information. Parents are always welcome in the library as volunteers for shelving and book fair.

Playground

was established in 1982

The Great Falls Playground is a fun place for students to play and hang out with their friends.

Computer Lab

was established in 1992

The Great Falls Computer Lab is a quiet place where students learn to code basic things and take important tests.
